# Why No Winner

No winner is awarded because no repo crossed from product-shaped readiness into investment-grade proof.

## Product-Shaped Readiness

The strongest repos became real enough to run:

- they have product surfaces,
- they generate buyer-relevant artifacts,
- they disclose mocks,
- they pass tests and evals,
- they produce receipts,
- they can be explained to a target buyer.

That is a meaningful result. It is also not the same as fundability.

## Investment-Grade Proof

A fictional $10M seed check requires evidence that survives contact with reality:

- a real target user reviewed the output,
- real or public input data went through the product,
- the generated artifact was judged useful,
- someone asked for a pilot, sample-data run, follow-up, or paid use,
- the product changed a buyer's workflow or decision.

None of the repos proved that yet.

## The Missing Bar

The missing bar is external proof/customer evidence.

The arena can no longer increase conviction through local Codex work alone. More features, prettier demos, more generated packets, or another internal judge would mostly create fake certainty.

## Decision

No $10M winner is awarded.

The only valid reopening condition is external evidence tied to a specific repo: a real customer, real data source, or real use case that can produce a credible proof packet.
