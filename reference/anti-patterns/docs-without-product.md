# Anti-Pattern: Docs Without Product

## What It Looks Like

The repo has thoughtful strategy, PRDs, investor memos, roadmaps, and risk docs, but the runnable product is thin, broken, or missing.

## Why It Fails

In this arena, narrative cannot compensate for product reality. A startup cannot win by describing the product it wishes it had built.

## How To Detect It

- Markdown quality exceeds product quality.
- Claims in docs are not visible in `make demo`.
- The strongest artifacts are memos, not generated product outputs.
- The repo passes narrative review but fails technical diligence.

## How To Fix It

Translate the strongest claims into product behavior. Every claimed improvement should have a runnable demo, test, eval, generated artifact, or receipt proving it exists.
