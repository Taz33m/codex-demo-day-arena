# Anti-Pattern: Generic AI Dashboard

## What It Looks Like

The product is a dashboard with chat, cards, charts, and generic AI summaries, but the workflow could apply to almost any customer or domain.

## Why It Fails

Generic dashboards hide weak wedge thinking. Investors and buyers need to see the specific painful workflow being improved, not a configurable surface waiting for a use case.

## How To Detect It

- The persona is broad or vague.
- The page has many panels but no clear operator sequence.
- The generated outputs are generic summaries.
- The product could be renamed for another market with little change.

## How To Fix It

Pick one operator, one painful workflow, and one buyer-relevant artifact. Remove panels that do not advance that workflow. The demo should make the wedge obvious within 60 seconds.
