# Buyer Judging

Buyer judging asks whether a target operator would care about the output.

## Judge Questions

- Would the target buyer care?
- What product output is actually useful?
- What feels like demo theater?
- What is the strongest reason to believe?
- What is the strongest reason to pass?
- Should the product receive more compute?

## Outcome

The buyer judge can mark a repo buyer-compelling while still requiring external proof. That is the intended distinction between product taste and market evidence.
