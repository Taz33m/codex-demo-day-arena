# Gate Definitions

| Gate | Required evidence | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Structural readiness | Runnable demo, tests, evals, receipt, product surface, buyer artifact, mock disclosure. | Candidate is judgeable. |
| Buyer usefulness | Score, verdict, recommendation, useful output, strongest believe/pass reasons, demo-theater critique. | Candidate is plausible under synthetic buyer review. |
| Compute allocation | Validator readiness plus buyer usefulness plus no external-proof blocker. | Another owner round may change an investment belief. |
| External proof | Real/public input data, target-user feedback, pilot request, sample-data request, or willingness-to-pay signal. | Candidate can approach investment review. |
| IC decision | Technical maturity, buyer usefulness, external proof, and comparative portfolio judgment. | Candidate may receive the fictional $10M check. |
